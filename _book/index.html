Welcome

Hello and welcome to the industry club of the Department of Computer Science at the University of Manchester. Membership of this club is open to employers who:

  • Recruit our students
    • undergraduate (Bachelors degrees) postgraduate degrees (Masters and PhD)
  • Collaborate on research projects
  • Participate in other activities such as our software engineering mentoring scheme and careers fairs
